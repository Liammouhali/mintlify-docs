## Overview

Kakiyo integrations allow you to connect external lead generation platforms directly to your campaigns. When these platforms capture leads, they automatically flow into Kakiyo as prospects, ready for your AI-powered outreach.

## How Integrations Work

1. **Connect** - Link your third-party account to Kakiyo
2. **Configure** - Select which campaign should receive the leads
3. **Activate** - Add your webhook secret to verify incoming data
4. **Capture** - Leads flow automatically into your campaign as prospects
