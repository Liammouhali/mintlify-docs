> Manage your connected LinkedIn accounts, configure outreach settings, and monitor account performance. Each profile operates independently with dedicated security infrastructure.

## Profile Limits by Plan

- **Pioneer Plan**: 1 LinkedIn account
- **Hunter Plan**: Up to 3 LinkedIn accounts
- **Conqueror Plan**: Up to 5 LinkedIn accounts

---

## Outreach Configuration

### Scheduling Settings

- **Operating hours**: Set specific times for AI agent activity
- **Timezone configuration**: Match your geographical location
- **Daily schedules**: Define when outreach should occur
- **Weekly patterns**: Customize activity for different days

### Invitation Limits

- **Daily limits**: Adjustable between 15-60 invitations per day
- **Recommended range**: 30-40 invitations daily for account safety
- **Risk management**: Higher limits increase account restriction risk
- **Monthly limits**: Maximum 800 invitations per month (LinkedIn's guideline)

### Safety Guidelines

- **Stay within limits**: Don't exceed 40 invitations daily
- **Monitor account health**: Watch for any LinkedIn warnings
- **Gradual scaling**: Increase volume slowly over time
- **Account age consideration**: Newer accounts should start lower

## Profile Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics

- **Invitations sent**: Daily and total invitation counts
- **Messages sent**: Outbound message volume
- **Messages received**: Response and engagement tracking
- **Connection acceptance**: Rate of invitation approvals

### Performance Monitoring

- **Individual profiles**: Track each LinkedIn account separately
- **Comparative analysis**: Compare performance across profiles
- **Limit compliance**: Ensure staying within safe parameters

## Account Security

### Operational Protection

- **Managed runtime**: Each account runs in a secure, isolated environment
- **Location alignment**: Operations match your configured geographical region
- **Natural behavior patterns**: Activity pacing simulates authentic human usage

### Account Independence

- **Separate operation**: Each profile runs independently
- **No interference**: Personal LinkedIn usage unaffected
- **Secure credentials**: Encrypted storage of login information
- **Isolated activity**: Each account operates in its own environment

### Profile Maintenance
