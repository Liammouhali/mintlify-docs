> The AI Builder is Kakiyo's fastest way to get started. Instead of manually configuring every component, the AI Builder automatically sets up your account with personalized prompts and offerings based on your business information.

## What is AI Builder?

AI Builder is an intelligent setup wizard that creates a complete, ready-to-test Kakiyo configuration in minutes. It analyzes your business and automatically generates:

- **Personalized offering**: Based on your website or business description
- **Optimized prompts**: Context and first message prompts tailored to your goals
- **Campaign foundation**: Ready-to-launch campaign configuration
- **Testing environment**: Immediate access to Sandbox for conversation testing

---

## Two Setup Methods

### Method 1: Website Scraping

The fastest setup option that analyzes your existing website content.

**Process:**

- **Website Analysis**: Provide your website URL for automatic content scraping
- **Goal Definition**: Specify your primary objective (book meetings, generate leads, etc.)
- **Link Configuration**: Add your link related to the goal
- **Language Selection**: Choose your prospecting language
- **Automatic Generation**: AI creates personalized prompts and offering

**Best for:**

- Businesses with comprehensive websites
- Companies with clear value propositions online
- Users wanting the absolute fastest setup

### Method 2: Three-Question Setup

A guided approach for businesses preferring manual input over website scraping.

**Process:**

- **Business Questions**: Answer three strategic questions about your business
- **Goal Definition**: Specify your primary objective
- **Link Configuration**: Add your booking or action link
- **Language Selection**: Choose your prospecting language
- **Intelligent Creation**: AI builds customized configuration based on your responses

**Best for:**

- Businesses with multiple service offerings
- Companies wanting more control over messaging
- Users with complex or developing websites

## Common Questions

#### Can I modify AI Builder results?

Yes, everything created by AI Builder can be edited and refined after setup. Use it as a strong foundation that you can customize.

#### What if my website isn't comprehensive?

Choose the three-question method instead. It allows you to provide specific business details that might not be clear on your website.

#### How accurate is the AI analysis?

The AI Builder uses advanced models to analyze your business, but results improve with clear, detailed input. Always test in Sandbox before launching.

#### Can I start over if needed?

Yes, you can run AI Builder multiple times or switch to manual setup if the results don't meet your needs.
