Kakiyo is an AI-powered LinkedIn outreach platform that helps teams run personalized outbound conversations at scale.

It combines campaign automation, prompt control, lead discovery, and inbox management so you can move from prospecting to qualification in one workspace.

## What Kakiyo Helps You Do

- Launch and manage outbound campaigns
- Start and continue natural LinkedIn conversations
- Qualify prospects with consistent criteria
- Route qualified opportunities toward your next step (demo, meeting, handoff)

## Core Product Areas

- **Campaigns**: Build, pause, resume, and monitor outbound workflows
- **Prompts and Offerings**: Control messaging strategy and product context
- **Inbox**: Review conversations and switch between AI and manual control
- **Lead Finder**: Discover and import prospects directly into campaigns
- **Analytics**: Track acceptance, reply, and qualification trends

## Who Uses Kakiyo

- Founders and solo operators running outbound themselves
- Sales teams scaling LinkedIn outreach with clear guardrails
- Agencies managing multiple client workspaces and campaigns

## How to Start

1. Connect your LinkedIn account
2. Set up your offering and prompts
3. Create your first campaign
4. Import prospects by CSV or Lead Finder
5. Monitor and optimize results in Inbox and Analytics
